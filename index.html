Honolulu Real Property Tax Calculator

This simple app helps you estimate your annual real property taxes based on your assessment information. Enter values from your Notice of Assessment. Rates are based on fiscal year July 1, 2025 to June 30, 2026. This is an estimate only; consult official sources for accuracy.

Most Residential class properties have a home exemption, typically of $120,000. Enter it here if appropriate.

Tip: You can copy values directly from your assessment notice and paste them into the fields above for quick input.
